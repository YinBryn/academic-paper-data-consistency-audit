# Neutral Comment Draft

I would appreciate clarification regarding the activation-energy value reported for `Synthetic-Cathode-A`.

Using the Rp values listed at 800, 750, and 700 °C and fitting `ln(Rp)` against `1000/T`, I obtain an activation energy of approximately 1.33 eV. This appears to differ from the synthetic reported value of 0.85 eV.

Could the authors clarify whether a different fitting convention, data range, unit conversion, or correction factor was used for the reported value?
