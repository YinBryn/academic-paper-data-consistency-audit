# Neutral Comment Draft

I would appreciate clarification regarding the Faradaic efficiency value listed for `Synthetic-Electrolyzer-B`.

Using the listed current density of 0.5 A/cm² and active area of 1.0 cm² gives a total current of 0.5 A. With 2 electrons per product molecule, the measured product flow of 2.45 mL/min corresponds to a calculated FE of approximately 70.3% under the stated molar-volume convention. This appears to differ from the reported FE of 95.0%.

Could the authors clarify whether a different gas-volume convention, active area, current value, or product-flow correction was used?
