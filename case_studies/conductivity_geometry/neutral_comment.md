# Neutral Comment Draft

I would appreciate clarification regarding the conductivity value listed for `Synthetic-Electrolyte-B`.

Using the listed resistance of 10.0 ohm, thickness of 0.33 mm, and circular diameter of 6.0 mm, I calculate a conductivity of approximately 0.01167 S/cm using `sigma = L / (R × A)`. This appears to differ from the reported value of 0.02000 S/cm.

Could the authors clarify whether a different sample area, thickness, resistance value, or normalization convention was used?
