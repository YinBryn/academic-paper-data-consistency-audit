# Neutral Comment Draft

I would appreciate clarification regarding the Rp component values for `Synthetic-Electrode-B` at 600 °C.

The table reports a total Rp of 0.180 Ω·cm². However, the listed component values are 0.052, 0.061, and 0.038 Ω·cm², which sum to 0.151 Ω·cm². This appears to differ from the reported total by approximately 19.2%.

Could the authors clarify whether the total Rp includes an additional component, a different fitting range, or a separate normalization not listed in the component table?
